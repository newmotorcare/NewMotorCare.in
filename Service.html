A car garage is a place where people can bring their vehicles for repair, maintenance, or inspection. A car garage may also sell parts and accessories for cars, such as batteries, wipers, filters, or mats. A car garage is usually run by a car mechanic, who is a skilled professional who can diagnose and fix problems with cars. A car mechanic may use various tools and equipment, such as wrenches, jacks, scanners, or welders. A car mechanic may also have certifications or licenses to work on specific types of cars or systems.
